Paris, France

Cultural

Paris offers a lot of cultural activites: art exhibitions, opera performances, theatre plays, sports events, you name

Architectual

Paris is not only a cultural city, it is first and foremost an architectural jewel. In Paris, you can find beautiful Haussmann buildings, modern art deco buildings, magnificent churches - like the Notre Dame or Saint Eustache -as well as more unique pieces like the Louis Vuitton Foundation or the George Pompidou Centre.

Secret

Paris harbours secrets of all kinds: Catacombs, covered passages, hidden gardens, quarries, old arenas... You'll never find out all its secrets, but it's worth a try. The Garden of the Buddhist Pantheon in the 16th century is particularly noteworthy, it is a true haven of peace unknown to tourists and even to some locals.
